Sutanu Bhattacharya, Ph.D.


About me

I am joining Florida Polytechnic University in Fall 2021 as an Assistant Professor in Computer Science department.

I earned Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Auburn University, in August 2021. My advisor was Dr. Debswapna Bhattacharya. My dissertation title is New computational and data-driven methods for protein homology modeling (PDF).

My basic interest lies in protein three-dimensional (3D) structure prediction problem, specifically low-homology protein threading.


Research Interests

  • Bioinformatics and Computational Biology
  • Deep Learning
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
