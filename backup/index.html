About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Auburn University, since August 2017. My advisor is Dr. Debswapna Bhattacharya. My basic interest lies in template-based protein 3D struture prediction, specifically protein threading.


Research Interests

  • Bioinformatics and Computational Biology
  • Machine Learning
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
